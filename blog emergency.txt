<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>If</em>  <em>Waking up to the cold breeze with the aroma of coffee plantation and witnessing the scenic view of the beautiful hill station is on your bucket list for a long time, then its time for you to open your wardrobe and pack your bag with all the dresses that you had bought, keeping in mind that you will style up them on your vacation to some beautiful hill station</em>.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Coorg is the most beautiful and popular hill station of Karnataka state. It is also called <strong>'Kodagu'</strong> in Kannada. It is well known for its foggy landscape throughout the year. It is the most favorite honeymoon destination for newly married couples!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The scenic view of Coorg with the large lush green forest, spice plantation, waterfalls along with the aroma of fresh coffee plantation and nippy fog all over the hill station, makes your visit to Coorg worth your while.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The Best time to visit Coorg is between<strong> October and May. </strong>The weather in this time period is the best for site seeing and enjoying some adventurous activities like hiking, trekking, river rafting, etc. Now let us list out some of the Best Places<strong> to visit in Coorg</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"textAlign":"center"} -->
<h2 class="has-text-align-center"><strong>Dubare Elephant Camp</strong></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":66,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none","className":"is-style-default"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large is-style-default"><img src="https://dreameronboard.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/IMG_20200301_092053-1-768x1024.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-66"/><figcaption>Photo by dreameronboard</figcaption></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>If you are a person who loves to soak up with Mother Nature and love the interaction with Elephants then you must not miss an opportunity to visit this camp. You will thank me for recommending this place to you. <em>(OK!! Thank me later:) )</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>To reach the Elephant camp you have to cross the river Cauvery by a small ferry boat ride, which will cost you around<em> </em><strong><em>INR 60</em>.</strong> In the dry season, the boats will not be available, as the water level will be low...But here comes the most fun and adventurous part, that is crossing the river by walk!! </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>There are about 50 Elephants in the camp. They are very well maintained and well-fed. You can take part in the<em> <strong>bathing session</strong></em> of Elephants which is a lifetime experience which one should not miss. <strong>(The fees for the Bathing session is INR 100)</strong>. The elephants here are very friendly and are very well behaved.  The most appreciable thing in this camp is that the elephants are not chained but they are always under the supervision of their mahouts. I am sure you will carry a bunch of beautiful memories for your lifetime.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This place offers you other fun activities like Horse ride, River rafting, Kayaking, etc. There are many shopping stalls and eateries. You will find many <em>Homemade wine </em>shops on the way to the camp.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"textAlign":"center"} -->
<h2 class="has-text-align-center">Namdroling Monastery</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":74,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://dreameronboard.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/IMG_20200229_164659-1024x768.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-74"/><figcaption>Photo by dreameronboard</figcaption></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>It is also called as<strong> Golden templ</strong>e. It is<strong> <em>5km</em></strong> away from<em> Kushalnagara</em> and <em><strong>35 km</strong></em> from <em>Coorg city</em>. It is located in Bylakuppe. The moment you enter the Monastery, the Tibetan architecture fills your heart with bliss. The history of this place is fascinating! The whole monastery is beautiful and peaceful. You will find many monks and other worshippers offering their prayer and meditating. Of course, You also can try doing meditation and feel the silence of nature. (If not at least try to maintain the silence inside the campus).</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The visuals of the Golden Buddha statue inside the prayer hall are just breathtaking!!  You will get knowledge of Tibetan Buddhism culture. This place fills your heart with peace.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p> You will find Tibetan flags hung all over the place which looks beautiful. It is a perfect location to take some pictures. You will find many eateries and shops outside the monastery which makes your visit to this place memorable.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"textAlign":"center"} -->
<h2 class="has-text-align-center">Kaveri Nisargadhama</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":75,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://dreameronboard.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/IMG_20200229_162600-1024x768.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-75"/><figcaption>Photo by dreameronboard</figcaption></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>If you are planning a day out with kids and family then it is a perfect place for you. It is an island formed by the river Kaveri near Kushalnagara. It gives you a thrilling experience of being with nature and feel the forest. It is a small island(perhaps you call it a park), which hosts some of the beautiful flora and a small bamboo forest.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>You have to get the tickets for entering the park<em><strong>(Ticket charge INR 10)</strong></em>. As you enter, you have to walk over a beautiful hanging bridge which gives you a thrilling experience. The bridge is hung over a small stream of River Kaveri and the small boats floating on the water will add to the beauty of the park. </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>There is a small water body inside the park which very safe for children and adults to play and have fun. The park showcases some <em>sculptures of Kodava's culture</em>. The park has a very well maintained path for tourists to take a walk around the whole park. There is a<em> Deer Park</em> inside the island and those deer enjoy the interaction with people and kids. You can take part in some adventurous activities like peddling boats, rafting, etc. There are many eating points and a shopping complex outside the park.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"textAlign":"center"} -->
<h2 class="has-text-align-center">Raja's seat</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":78,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://dreameronboard.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/IMG_20201114_082605-1024x576.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-78"/><figcaption>Photo by dreameronboard</figcaption></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p> Raj's seat is one of the must-visit places in Coorg. This place has a history of the King of Kodagu, who had built it to watch the beautiful sunset and relax with his family. This place gives a panoramic view of the high and low rise mountains of the valley. Usually, this place is a bit crowded, especially in the evening.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong><em>The sunset that you witness here gives you goosebumps!</em></strong> There are benches installed for visitors to sit and enjoy the sunset with family and friends. It is a well-maintained park that has specific pathways for walkers. The beautiful flowers will make you feel energetic with their mesmerizing charm. The park has interesting sculptures of animals and birds with a beautiful musical fountain, which is an add on to the beauty of the park.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>There are<em> <strong>no entrance fees</strong> </em>to the park. It is an open park. Usually, this place is a bit crowded during the weekend and holidays. This place does not have many activities, it is just one of the sightseeing places in Coorg. <em><strong>It is a place, just to sit and enjoy the stunning view of the valley. </strong></em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"textAlign":"center"} -->
<h2 class="has-text-align-center">Abbey falls</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":89,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://dreameronboard.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/aaa-1024x603.png" alt="" class="wp-image-89"/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Abbey falls is one of the must-visit places in Coorg. <em>The water gushing from about 70 feet high, from the midst of lush green coffee plantation and some other spice plantation with the fresh aroma of coffee in the cool breeze gives you goosebumps!</em> The sound of the water makes your mind blank for a moment for sure. You will feel nice in the midst of the waterfall. <em><strong>The best time to visit this place is post-monsoon(November to December)</strong></em>. You can not swim or play in the water, you only could watch.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The falls are about<em><strong> 6 km from Coorg</strong></em> and about <strong><em>34 km from Kushalnagara</em></strong>. To reach the Abbey falls you can come by bus or can hire any private vehicles. To reach the viewing point of the falls you have to climb down for about 150 steps. <em>If you are traveling with the elders it might become difficult for them to climb up, It is a bit strenuous.</em> But if you are fit, do not miss a chance to enjoy the beauty of the river Kaveri gushing down the rocks!!</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"textAlign":"center"} -->
<h2 class="has-text-align-center">Sri Omkareshwara Temple</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><em>If you are in Coorg for a vacation, you can start your day by paying your first visit to the Omkareshwara temple</em>. It is a temple of Lord Shiva. The Shivalingam here was brought from Varanasi(Kashi). It was built in the year 1820 by King Lingarajendra.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>This temple has Islamic architecture and the walls of the temple have mythological paintings that make the place interesting. <em>The temple has a holy pond that has lots of holy fishes and turtles in it. Just sit and enjoy the calmness and get the positive vibes.</em></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The temple is very well maintained. This is a perfect place to sit and meditate. <em>The temple is very calm and divine</em>. you can offer your prayer to Lord Shiva and enjoy the '<em>prasadam'</em> given in the temple. There is one thing to note, <em><strong>the traditional dress code is mandatory to enter the temple</strong></em>. The temple is very near to the main city of Coorg.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"textAlign":"center"} -->
<h2 class="has-text-align-center">Mallalli Falls</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":90,"width":578,"height":325,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large is-resized"><img src="https://dreameronboard.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/IMG_20170716_133334-1024x576.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-90" width="578" height="325"/><figcaption>Photo by dreameronboard</figcaption></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Mallalli falls is situated in the northern part of the Coorg district.<em><strong> </strong>It is about <strong>30 km from Coorg </strong>and<strong> 48km from Kushalnagar. </strong></em>It is situated in the Pushpagiri hills. The main water source for this waterfall is the Kumaradhara river which flows to the Arabian sea via Kukke Subrahmanya and Mangalore. To witness the water gushing from 120 feet over huge rocks is just mindblowing !!  The best time to visit this place is from<strong><em> July-August. </em></strong>The roads to the falls are very narrow and adventurous, be careful while driving your vehicles.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>You can enjoy the view of the falls from the top as well as from the bottom of the falls. To reach the bottom of the falls you have to be willing to trek down for about 600 steps, You have to be fit and healthy otherwise it is very strenuous. It is worth your while to visit the falls, the view from the bottom is spectacular!!<em><strong> It is not recommended for elders and children to go to the base</strong></em>. You have to carry some food or energy drink with you to be on the safer side.<em> <strong>Be very cautious while trekking, as there are no fences installed at some points</strong></em><strong>.</strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2>Nagarahole National Park</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"id":97,"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img src="https://dreameronboard.com/wp-content/uploads/2021/01/IMG_20201113_114436-1024x576.jpg" alt="" class="wp-image-97"/><figcaption>Photo by dreameronboard</figcaption></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>In childhood, I had always created my own imaginations about the forest and wildlife. I love forests, mountains, rivers, and wild animals.  If you have ever dreamt of experiencing the wildlife in a beautiful forest, then you should make it a priority to visit this place if you are in Coorg. The mesmerizing beauty of mother nature will leave you stunned! </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The main attraction of this place is<strong><em> the Jungle safari</em></strong>. The Forest department of Karnataka state and some private organizations hosts jungle safari. There are two types of safari; you can choose either<strong><em> Bus safari or Jeep safari.</em></strong> You will be accompanied by a driver come guide to the safari. They are very courteous and friendly. They know very brief knowledge about the national park.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Nagarahole hosts for wast flora and fauna. <em> Indian tigers, Asiatic elephants, sloth bears, leopards, spotted deers, wild dogs, sambar, hyena, and different species of birds and reptiles and amphibians are very easily seen</em>. If you are lucky enough you will spot the rare black panther! Your visit to this place will not be wasted for sure.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p><strong><em>Timings: 6 AM to 8 AM and 3 PM to 5 PM  (Safari timings might differ according to the weather)                                                                 </em></strong>      </p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>The best time to visit the jungle is between October to May. The park might remain closed during the rainy season. Before visiting the park make sure to contact the forest department of Nagarahole national park, and confirm the safari timings and charges. The safari might get canceled if there is a change in the weather.<strong><em> The entry fees are about INR 200 for Indians and INR 1000 for Foreigners.</em></strong></p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<h2>Harangi dam</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:image {"sizeSlug":"large","linkDestination":"none"} -->
<figure class="wp-block-image size-large"><img/></figure>
<!-- /wp:image -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Harangi dam built across the river Kaveri.It is about about 33 km from Coorg and 10 km from Kushalnagar.The dam is very huge and give a stunning panoramic view of backwaters of the River kaveri in the midst of up and low rise hills  from the bridge.It is the first dam that is built across the river kaveri.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>It is a perfect place for a day out with kids and family. The park here have very well maintained pathways, which takes you to the dam gates, which give you a stunning view of outcoming water.There are no much activities to do here.If you are looking for a less crowded and peaceful place then you can definitely go here, as it is not much crowded.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul><li>Timings: 8 AM - 6 PM</li><li>Entry Fees: INR 10 for adults and INR 5 for kids.</li></ul>
